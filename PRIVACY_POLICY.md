# Privacy Policy for MySaga

**Last Updated:** March 31, 2026

MySaga ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the MySaga Chrome Extension and its associated backend services.

By using MySaga, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
To provide a persistent journaling experience, we require users to create an account. When you register, we collect:
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation, login, and communication.
- **Name:** Used to personalize your AI companion interactions.

### B. Personal Communications / User Content
The core purpose of MySaga is to act as a private journal with AI companionship. Because of this, we collect:
- **Journal Entries:** The text, emotional tags, and metadata you submit via the extension.
- **AI Conversations:** Messages sent between you and your AI companions.
- **Goals and Tasks:** Actionable items extracted from your journal entries by the AI.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data **strictly for providing the MySaga service**. Specifically, we use your data to:
- Authenticate your account and sync your journal across devices.
- Generate personalized feedback, emotional insights, and proactive check-ins from our AI companions via Large Language Models (LLMs).
- Map your entries into structured, long-term memory (EverMemOS) so your AI companions can remember past context and struggles.

**We do NOT sell your personal data or journal entries to any third parties.**

---

## 3. Third-Party Data Processors

To provide intelligent AI companionship and memory capabilities, MySaga transiently processes your encrypted journal text through trusted third-party artificial intelligence providers:

- **EverMemOS:** Used to securely extract and store long-term structured memory.
- **OpenRouter (LLMs):** Used to generate immediate insights, goals, and companion responses.

These providers act as data processors and only have access to the data necessary to perform these specific tasks. They are strictly prohibited from using your journal entries to train their generalized baseline models or for any marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Security & Storage

Your privacy is our utmost priority given the intimate nature of journaling.
- Your passwords are cryptographically hashed and never stored in plain text.
- All communications between the Chrome Extension and our servers are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
- We utilize industry-standard PostgreSQL databases for secure storage.

---

## 5. Your Data Rights & Portability

Because it is *your* story, you own your data. At any time, you have the right to:
- **Export Your Data:** You can download a complete JSON backup of all your journals directly from the extension settings.
- **Delete Your Account:** You can request the complete and permanent deletion of your account, which will permanently erase all journal entries, AI memories, and PII from our databases and third-party memory systems (EverMemOS).

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or if you wish to exercise your right to delete your data, please open an issue in this GitHub repository or contact the developer directly.
